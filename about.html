About Us

AICON Construction Supplies is a diversified conglomerate with four core businesses: all types of building materials, from hardware products to cement, gravel, and sands (aggregates), heavy equipment, construction machinery; general trade; procurement of request-specific products; and logistics. AICON is involved in the distribution and procurement of all kinds of construction materials. We ensure quality at a competitive price. With the help of our logistics, we also deliver the materials to the project sites with care so they will be used as new without damaging them. We understand the challenges and needs of transporting building materials from leading manufacturers to construction sites.

Mission

  • To provide professional building material advisory, customer-centric performance, and support to the industry.
  • To develop innovative, high-impact products and solutions that create value for our customers.
  • To provide affordable pricing and top-notch quality service to meet the demands of the market.

Vision

To be the Philippines' Leading Innovative Building Materials Specialist.

Core Values

  • Be the light, stay focused, and uphold integrity.
  • Teamwork Makes Dreams Work Never Give Up!
